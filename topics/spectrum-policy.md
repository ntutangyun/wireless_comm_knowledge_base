## Current state (EN)

This bin covers the **regulatory layer** of Wi-Fi: 6 GHz unlicensed rollouts, the upper-6 GHz contention with mobile operators, AFC (Automated Frequency Coordination) deployments, and academic work on coexistence with cellular bands (NR-U). Spectrum policy decisions are slow but irreversibly shape what Wi-Fi can do where — once a regulator carves a band for mobile-only or shared use, the radio side has to live with that for a generation.

2026 has been an active regulatory year, especially in Europe. The **EU RSPG decision** on the upper 6 GHz band (`2026-05-04_eu-rspg-upper-6ghz-decision`) allocated 540 MHz to mobile and froze 160 MHz for shared / future use — a defeat for the "all upper-6 to Wi-Fi" position. **Ofcom's "Better Together" plan** for the UK (`2026-05-04_ofcom-better-together-6ghz-uk`) takes a sharing approach, treating Wi-Fi and mobile as concurrent users of the same band, with technical sharing rules to be specified. On the academic side, two mechanism papers tackle Wi-Fi / NR-U coexistence: a DRL-based framework with three reward-shape policies (`2026-05-04_arxiv-policy-driven-drl-nru-wifi-coexistence`) and an LLM-orchestrated alpha-fairness approach (`2026-05-01_arxiv-llm-6ghz-wifi-nru-coexistence`).

Watch for: AFC pilot results from the FCC and equivalents; technical specifications of the Ofcom sharing rules (the policy says "share"; the *protocol* mechanism for sharing is unsettled); Asia-Pacific 6 GHz decisions (where the regulatory map is still drawing); APAC and India unlicensed extensions for low-power IoT.

## Current state (ZH)

本桶覆盖 Wi-Fi 的**监管层**：6 GHz 免许可推行、Upper-6 GHz 与移动运营商的争用、AFC（自动频率协调）部署，以及与蜂窝频段（NR-U）共存的学术工作。频谱政策决策缓慢但不可逆地塑造 Wi-Fi 能在何处做什么——监管者一旦把某个频段划为仅移动或共享使用，射频侧就要承受一代人的时间。

2026 年是监管活跃年，尤其在欧洲。**欧盟 RSPG 关于 upper 6 GHz 的决定**（`2026-05-04_eu-rspg-upper-6ghz-decision`）将 540 MHz 分给移动业务，160 MHz 冻结待共享 / 未来使用——是"upper-6 全归 Wi-Fi"立场的失利。**Ofcom 在英国的 "Better Together" 计划**（`2026-05-04_ofcom-better-together-6ghz-uk`）采取共享路线，把 Wi-Fi 与移动视为同频段并发用户，技术共享规则待指定。学界两篇机制论文研究 Wi-Fi / NR-U 共存：基于 DRL 且含三种奖励策略变体的框架（`2026-05-04_arxiv-policy-driven-drl-nru-wifi-coexistence`），以及 LLM 协调的 alpha 公平性方案（`2026-05-01_arxiv-llm-6ghz-wifi-nru-coexistence`）。

待关注：FCC 及对应监管机构的 AFC 试点结果；Ofcom 共享规则的技术规范（政策已说"共享"，但*协议*机制尚未敲定）；亚太地区的 6 GHz 决定（监管地图仍在绘制）；面向低功耗 IoT 的亚太及印度免许可扩展。
