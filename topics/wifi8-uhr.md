## Current state (EN)

This is the **802.11bn / Wi-Fi 8 / UHR umbrella** — work that touches the standard at a level not better described by a more specific bin (`mlo`, `mapc-cosr`, `rtwt-latency`, etc.). It absorbs draft-progress readouts, plenary / interim digests, vendor positioning announcements that are about "Wi-Fi 8" generically rather than a specific mechanism, and the AIML / WNG / AI Offload Study Group activity that does not yet have its own task group.

The 2026 schedule is the load-bearing fact for this bin. As of May 2026: 802.11bn Draft 1.3 was approved at the January Victoria interim with ~60 % of LB291 comments resolved (`2026-05-01_ieee-january-2026-readout`); March 2026 plenary closed without ratifying D2.0 and stood up the AI Offload Study Group (`2026-05-01_ieee-march-2026-readout`); the **D2.0 ballot has slipped from May to July 2026** while the May 2028 ratification target remains intact (`2026-05-01_ieee-tgbn-d2-schedule`). The May 2026 Antwerp interim is in flight; the Mentor pre-interim digest (`2026-05-03_ieee-mentor-may-2026-pre-interim-digest`) lists the contributions to expect. Vendor positioning is dense: Qualcomm's MWC 2026 AI-native portfolio (`2026-05-01_qualcomm-wifi8-mwc2026`), Broadcom's CES 2026 unified platform (`2026-05-01_broadcom-wifi8-ces2026`), MediaTek's Filogic 8000 (`2026-05-01_mediatek-filogic8000-ces2026`), Intel's four-pillar WWC vision (`2026-05-04_intel-wwc-wifi8-vision`), Gemtek / Quectel's second-tier announcements (`2026-05-01_gemtek-quectel-wifi8-mwc2026`). Backbone reading: the comprehensive 25-year Wi-Fi tutorial that culminates in 802.11bn (`2026-05-01_arxiv-25-years-tutorial`), and the AIML CSI feedback compression survey (`2026-05-01_arxiv-aiml-csi-feedback-compression`) covering the IEEE 802.11 AIML TIG.

Watch for: the July 2026 D2.0 ballot outcome (the next big inflection); how AI Offload Study Group's mandate shapes — whether it produces a new task group or stays exploratory; WNG / Wi-Fi 9 contributions starting to appear in Mentor with concrete mechanism proposals.

## Current state (ZH)

这是 **802.11bn / Wi-Fi 8 / UHR 总览**——所有触及该标准、但更细桶（`mlo`、`mapc-cosr`、`rtwt-latency` 等）描述不准确的工作的归属。它吸纳草案进度综述、全会 / 中期会议摘要、关于"Wi-Fi 8"整体而非具体机制的厂商定位公告，以及尚无独立任务组的 AIML / WNG / AI Offload 研究组活动。

2026 年的时间表是本桶的承重事实。截至 2026 年 5 月：802.11bn D1.3 在 1 月维多利亚中期会议获批，LB291 评审完成约 60%（`2026-05-01_ieee-january-2026-readout`）；3 月全会未通过 D2.0、并成立了 AI Offload 研究组（`2026-05-01_ieee-march-2026-readout`）；**D2.0 投票已由 5 月推迟至 2026 年 7 月**，而 2028 年 5 月的批准目标仍保留（`2026-05-01_ieee-tgbn-d2-schedule`）。2026 年 5 月安特卫普中期会议正在进行；Mentor 中期前摘要（`2026-05-03_ieee-mentor-may-2026-pre-interim-digest`）列出了将出现的贡献。厂商定位密集：Qualcomm 在 MWC 2026 的 AI-Native 产品组合（`2026-05-01_qualcomm-wifi8-mwc2026`）、Broadcom 在 CES 2026 的统一平台（`2026-05-01_broadcom-wifi8-ces2026`）、MediaTek Filogic 8000（`2026-05-01_mediatek-filogic8000-ces2026`）、Intel 在 WWC 的四支柱愿景（`2026-05-04_intel-wwc-wifi8-vision`）、Gemtek / Quectel 等二线公告（`2026-05-01_gemtek-quectel-wifi8-mwc2026`）。背景阅读：汇总至 802.11bn 的 25 年 Wi-Fi 综述（`2026-05-01_arxiv-25-years-tutorial`），以及覆盖 IEEE 802.11 AIML TIG 的 AIML CSI 反馈压缩综述（`2026-05-01_arxiv-aiml-csi-feedback-compression`）。

待关注：2026 年 7 月 D2.0 投票结果（下一个重大拐点）；AI Offload 研究组的范围如何成形——是产生新任务组还是停留在探索阶段；WNG / Wi-Fi 9 贡献开始以具体机制提案形式出现在 Mentor 中。
