## Summary (EN)

Wi-Fi 7 has crossed the 500-million certified-device-shipment mark by late April 2026, with the Wireless Broadband Alliance (WBA) projecting cumulative shipments to exceed 1.5 billion units by end-2026. That places Wi-Fi 7 ahead of any prior generation's 30-month adoption curve. The WBA also released results from a multi-vendor enterprise-deployment trial — conducted with **AT&T, CommScope, and Intel** — that quantifies real-world performance gains over Wi-Fi 6: significant improvements in throughput, airtime efficiency, and (most importantly for procurement) **95th- and 99th-percentile latency consistency**.

The latency-consistency framing is the more interesting half of the story. Buyers are now demanding proof of tail-latency stability, **not** peak speed, as the headline metric — a shift driven by XR, cloud gaming, and real-time-collaboration deployments where the 99th percentile is what users feel. The WBA also rolled out **Wi-Fi Device Metrics**, a standardised testing methodology and terminology so that throughput, latency, and user-experience claims across vendors can be compared apples-to-apples.

For practitioners, this is the most useful publication of the cycle: a vendor-neutral measurement framework and the first generation-scale deployment data that anchors what "good Wi-Fi 7" means in the field.

## Summary (ZH)

到 2026 年 4 月底，Wi-Fi 7 累计认证设备出货已突破 5 亿台，无线宽带联盟（WBA）预测到 2026 年底累计出货量将超过 15 亿台 —— 其 30 个月的采用曲线领先此前任何一代。WBA 同时发布了一项多厂商企业部署试验的结果（**AT&T、CommScope、Intel**联合参与），量化了相对 Wi-Fi 6 的实际性能提升：吞吐与空中时间效率有显著改善，更重要的是采购方所关心的 **p95 / p99 时延一致性**。

时延一致性才是这条新闻的更有意思一半。买家现在要求厂商证明的是尾时延稳定性，**而非**峰值速率 —— 这是由 XR、云游戏、实时协作等部署场景驱动的转变，因为这些场景里用户能感知到的是第 99 百分位。WBA 同时推出了 **Wi-Fi Device Metrics**，一套标准化的测试方法与术语，使得不同厂商之间在吞吐、时延、用户体验等指标上的声明可以做"同口径对比"。

对从业者而言，这是本代周期里最有用的一次发布：一个厂商中立的测量框架，加上首批代际规模的部署数据，从此固化了实地"好的 Wi-Fi 7"应该是什么样。

## Key technical points (EN)

- **Cumulative certified-device shipments:** ≥ 500 M by late April 2026
- **Projected 2026 year-end:** > 1.5 B cumulative units
- **Trial partners:** AT&T (operator), CommScope (infrastructure), Intel (silicon)
- **Improvement axes (vs Wi-Fi 6):** throughput, efficiency, **p95 / p99 latency consistency**
- **Wi-Fi Device Metrics framework** released — standard methodology + terminology for cross-vendor measurement
- **Latency consistency** has displaced peak speed as the primary procurement criterion in enterprise

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)

- **First generation-scale outcome data for Wi-Fi 7.** Until now Wi-Fi 7 commentary was forward-looking. The WBA results are real-world enterprise-deployment numbers that 802.11be marketing claims can be measured against.
- **Tail-latency-as-headline.** The shift to p95 / p99 as the primary procurement metric has implications for the *next* generation: TGbn's three headline targets (throughput +25%, p95 latency −25%, MPDU loss −25% — see `2026-05-01_ieee-tgbn-d2-schedule.md`) are explicitly aligned with what enterprise buyers are now asking for. The market and the standards body are converging.
- **Wi-Fi Device Metrics is a measurement-tooling reference for any subsequent paper or product claim.** Worth treating as the companion to BLADE (`2026-05-01_arxiv-blade-adaptive-contention.md`) and the bf-ax-coexistence ns-3 module (`2026-05-01_arxiv-bf-ax-coexistence-ns3.md`) — three different layers of the measurement stack.

- **Wi-Fi 7 首批代际规模的实地数据。** 此前关于 Wi-Fi 7 的评论都是前瞻性。WBA 的结果是真实企业部署的数字，从此 802.11be 的市场宣称有了可对照基线。
