## Summary (EN)

Wi-Fi Alliance announced at CES 2026 (January 2026) a new Wi-Fi CERTIFIED 7 certification track specifically for **client devices that operate only on 20 MHz channels**. The certification carves a low-complexity, low-power IoT-friendly path into the otherwise wideband-centric Wi-Fi 7 ecosystem. It targets battery-powered sensors, smart-home devices, wearables, and other low-data-rate endpoints that don't need the 320 MHz / multi-band capability the rest of the standard is built around but still want the modern feature set (improved scheduling, MLO awareness, security).

The 20-MHz-only profile gives such devices three concrete advantages: lower **power consumption** (narrower channels need less RF and DSP), simpler **RF design** (cheaper, more compact silicon), and better **link reliability** in dense deployments where a 20 MHz client is less exposed to wideband interference. The certification preserves Wi-Fi 7's core multi-user benefits — multiple devices can transmit upstream simultaneously without waiting in line — which is the part of the standard that actually matters in dense IoT scenarios.

The announcement formally creates a "low-end Wi-Fi 7" product category. Vendors that have been hesitant to put full-bandwidth Wi-Fi 7 chipsets into low-cost IoT silicon now have a defined certification target that fits the cost / power envelope.

## Summary (ZH)

Wi-Fi 联盟在 2026 年 1 月的 CES 2026 上发布了一项新的 Wi-Fi CERTIFIED 7 认证子类，**专为仅在 20 MHz 信道工作的客户端设备**而设。该认证在以宽带为中心的 Wi-Fi 7 生态中开辟出一条低复杂度、低功耗、对 IoT 友好的路径，目标对象是电池供电传感器、智能家居设备、可穿戴设备以及其他低数据率终端 —— 它们用不上标准本身围绕的 320 MHz / 多频段能力，但仍希望享受现代特性（更好的调度、MLO 意识、安全性）。

仅 20 MHz 的子类为这类设备带来三项具体优势：更低的**功耗**（窄带所需 RF 与 DSP 更少）、更简洁的**RF 设计**（芯片更便宜、更紧凑）、以及在密集部署下更好的**链路可靠性**（20 MHz 客户端对宽带干扰暴露更小）。该认证仍保留 Wi-Fi 7 的核心多用户优势 —— 多设备可以同时上行而不必排队 —— 这正是密集 IoT 场景中真正关键的能力。

本次发布正式确立了"低端 Wi-Fi 7"产品类别。此前对在低成本 IoT 硅片中加入完整带宽 Wi-Fi 7 芯片心存犹豫的厂商，现在有了一个匹配成本 / 功耗包络的认证目标。

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)

- **Splits the Wi-Fi 7 product line.** Until now Wi-Fi 7 was implicitly "wideband + multi-band". The 20-MHz cert formalises a low-end branch and lowers the activation energy for IoT silicon vendors to pick up Wi-Fi 7 features.
- **Locks in the IoT segment for Wi-Fi 7 ahead of Wi-Fi 8.** Without this certification, low-end IoT was likely to either skip Wi-Fi 7 or wait for a future low-power Wi-Fi 8 profile. The certification creates a real upgrade path *now*.
- **Scout signal:** the next 12-18 months should show low-cost Wi-Fi 7 IoT chips from MediaTek, Espressif, and similar IoT-leaning vendors. Track those when they appear.

## Why it matters / what's new (ZH)

- **将 Wi-Fi 7 产品线进行分层。** 此前 Wi-Fi 7 隐含定位为"宽带 + 多频段"。20 MHz 认证正式确立了低端分支，并降低 IoT 硅片厂商采用 Wi-Fi 7 特性的启动门槛。
- **在 Wi-Fi 8 到来之前为 IoT 板块锁定 Wi-Fi 7。** 没有该认证，低端 IoT 很可能会跳过 Wi-Fi 7 或等到未来的低功耗 Wi-Fi 8 子类。该认证*现在*就提供了真实的升级路径。
- **巡检线索：** 未来 12-18 个月应该会看到联发科、Espressif 等 IoT 倾向厂商推出低成本 Wi-Fi 7 IoT 芯片。出现时建议重点跟踪。
