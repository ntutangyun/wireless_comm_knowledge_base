## Summary (EN)
Two FCC actions in early 2026 reshaped the regulatory ceiling for SpaceX's Starlink. In January 2026 the FCC authorized SpaceX to deploy an additional 7,500 Gen2 Starlink satellites, lifting the total approved Gen2 constellation to 15,000 — the headroom Starlink needs to scale both its broadband backbone and its rapidly growing Direct-to-Cell layer. By early 2026 Starlink had already launched more than 650 direct-to-cell satellites, with the service live in 22 countries and reachable by over 400 million people, which SpaceX characterizes as the largest 4G network by coverage area on the planet.

The second action cut the other way: on 23 April 2026 the FCC declined — at least for now — SpaceX's bid to access additional satellite spectrum for direct-to-device service. The denial signals that regulators are pacing spectrum grants amid contested demand (AST SpaceMobile was cleared two days earlier on lower-band 698–960 MHz cellular spectrum) and interference concerns from terrestrial and adjacent satellite operators. Together the two decisions show the FCC expanding Starlink's *satellite count* while holding the line on *new spectrum*, forcing direct-to-cell capacity growth to come from constellation density and reused partner-MNO bands rather than fresh allocations.

For the KB this anchors the NGSO-regulation bin on the U.S. side and pairs with the AST full-constellation approval as the two halves of the 2026 direct-to-device regulatory picture.

2026 年初 FCC 的两项行动重塑了 SpaceX 星链的监管上限。2026 年 1 月，FCC 批准 SpaceX 再部署 7,500 颗 Gen2 星链卫星，使获批的 Gen2 星座总数升至 15,000 颗——这正是星链扩展其宽带骨干与快速增长的 Direct-to-Cell 层所需的余量。到 2026 年初，星链已发射逾 650 颗直连蜂窝卫星，服务在 22 个国家上线、可触达逾 4 亿人，SpaceX 称其为全球覆盖面积最大的 4G 网络。

第二项行动方向相反：2026 年 4 月 23 日，FCC（至少暂时）驳回了 SpaceX 为直连终端服务申请额外卫星频谱的请求。该驳回表明监管者在需求竞争（AST SpaceMobile 两天前获批使用低频段 698–960 MHz 蜂窝频谱）与地面及邻近卫星运营商干扰顾虑下，正在控制频谱授予节奏。两项决定合起来表明：FCC 扩大星链的*卫星数量*，但在*新频谱*上守住底线，迫使直连蜂窝的容量增长来自星座密度与复用合作运营商频段，而非新增分配。

对本知识库而言，本条目锚定 NGSO 监管分箱的美国侧，并与 AST 完整星座批准一起，构成 2026 直连终端监管图景的两半。
