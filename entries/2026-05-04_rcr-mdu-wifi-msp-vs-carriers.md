## Summary (EN)

In a May 4 2026 RCR Wireless analyst piece, **Adlane Fellah (Chief Analyst, Maravedis Research)** argues that the major US broadband carriers — Comcast Xfinity (31.25M subs), Charter Spectrum (29.7M), AT&T (~16.7M), Verizon (~13M) — **don't dominate the multi-dwelling-unit (MDU) Wi-Fi opportunity** despite their subscriber scale, and that managed-Wi-Fi MSPs are winning the segment on operational service delivery. Roughly nine out of ten new US multifamily development projects choose **managed Wi-Fi** over retail-ISP-per-unit models.

The structural reason: MDU connectivity is fundamentally an in-building operational problem (lease-up speed, RF design across thousands of units in a single asset, integration with PropTech access-control / IoT, tenant-roaming across the property), not a national-network-reach problem. Independent MSPs — Dojo Networks, Gigstreem, Elauwit, Zentro — operate at building-asset scale (Zentro ~106,000 units; Elauwit 25,000+ across 25 states) and offer single-vendor accountability, instant connectivity at lease signing, and property-wide roaming on a single SSID without per-unit equipment.

The piece also flags the upcoming **Charter + Cox merger (~35.6–38M subs combined)** as a 2026 consolidation event that nonetheless changes little about the MDU dynamic — household-level subscriber counts don't translate into building-asset operational capability. Elauwit cites a **200-300-basis-point NOI lift** for property owners that consolidate per-unit ISP circuits into a managed-network model.

## Summary (ZH)

在 2026 年 5 月 4 日的 RCR Wireless 分析专栏中，**Maravedis Research 首席分析师 Adlane Fellah** 论证：尽管美国主要宽带运营商体量巨大——Comcast Xfinity（3125 万用户）、Charter Spectrum（2970 万）、AT&T（约 1670 万）、Verizon（约 1300 万）——**它们并不主导多户公寓（MDU）Wi-Fi 机会**；托管 Wi-Fi MSP 正凭运营服务交付能力赢下这个细分市场。美国新建多户公寓项目中约有九成选择 **托管 Wi-Fi** 模式，而非"每户一份零售 ISP"模式。

结构性原因：MDU 连接本质上是 *楼内运营* 问题（出租速度、单一资产里数千户的 RF 设计、与 PropTech 出入控制 / IoT 的集成、租户跨整栋物业的漫游），不是 *全国网络覆盖* 问题。独立 MSP——Dojo Networks、Gigstreem、Elauwit、Zentro——以"单栋资产"为运营单位（Zentro 约 10.6 万户；Elauwit 横跨 25 州、2.5 万户以上），提供单一供应商问责、签约即开通、全物业范围内单一 SSID 漫游（无需每户设备）。

文章还提到 **Charter + Cox 合并案（合计约 3560 万–3800 万用户）** 是 2026 年的整合事件，但对 MDU 局势影响有限——家庭级订户计数并不能转化为单栋资产的运营能力。Elauwit 数据表明，把每户分别接入的 ISP 电路整合为托管网络模式，可为业主带来 **200–300 个基点的 NOI 提升**。

## Key technical points (EN)

- **Top-10 broadband operator scale (Q4 2025):** Comcast 31.25M, Charter 29.7M, AT&T ~16.7M, Verizon ~13M (16.3M post-Frontier).
- **MDU choice ratio:** ~9 of 10 new US multifamily projects choose managed Wi-Fi (single vendor, property-wide) over retail-ISP-per-unit.
- **MSP scale (asset-level):** Zentro ~106,000 units; Elauwit 25,000+ units across 25 states.
- **Owner-side ROI:** Elauwit cites 200-300 bps NOI lift from converting per-unit ISP circuits into a managed network.
- **MSP service primitives:** instant connectivity at lease signing; property-wide roaming on single SSID; PropTech-integrated access control / IoT; professional RF spectrum management.
- **2026 consolidation:** Charter + Cox merger creates ~35.6–38M-subscriber operator; minimal MDU impact because household scale ≠ building-asset operations.

## Key technical points (ZH)

- **前十大宽带运营商规模（2025 Q4）：** Comcast 3125 万、Charter 2970 万、AT&T ~1670 万、Verizon ~1300 万（并 Frontier 后 1630 万）。
- **MDU 选择比例：** 约 9/10 的美国新建多户项目选择托管 Wi-Fi（单供应商、整栋范围），而非"每户一家零售 ISP"。
- **MSP 规模（资产口径）：** Zentro 约 10.6 万户；Elauwit 横跨 25 州、超 2.5 万户。
- **业主侧 ROI：** Elauwit 数据：把每户单独接入 ISP 改为托管网络，NOI 可提升 200–300 个基点。
- **MSP 服务原语：** 签约即开通；全物业单一 SSID 漫游；PropTech 集成的访问控制 / IoT；专业 RF 频谱管理。
- **2026 整合：** Charter + Cox 合并形成 3560 万–3800 万用户的运营商；对 MDU 局势影响有限——家庭口径 ≠ 单栋资产运营能力。

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)

The KB has tracked the enterprise-and-carrier sides of the Wi-Fi 7 deployment story (`cisco-wifi7-breakout-year-2026`, `extreme-networks-wifi7-momentum-q3`, `realtek-wifi7-revenue-surge-q1-2026`, `wifi7-deployment-milestone-2026`) but has had **zero coverage of the MDU / managed-Wi-Fi-service-provider tier**. That tier is materially different — the buying centre is the property owner / developer, not the IT / network team — and the design constraints (property-wide single SSID + roaming, PropTech integration, RF survey across hundreds of units) drive a distinct silicon and feature-set demand that does not map cleanly onto either consumer routers or enterprise APs. The piece establishes the segment's scale (≈90% of new US multifamily new-builds), names the players, and supplies a defensible economic mechanic (200-300 bps NOI lift). Combined with the carrier-MSO scale data, it becomes a baseline reference for *who actually buys Wi-Fi 7 / Wi-Fi 8 APs in residential property* and *what they buy on*.

## Why it matters / what's new (ZH)

KB 已经跟踪了 Wi-Fi 7 部署故事的企业侧与运营商侧（`cisco-wifi7-breakout-year-2026`、`extreme-networks-wifi7-momentum-q3`、`realtek-wifi7-revenue-surge-q1-2026`、`wifi7-deployment-milestone-2026`），但 **完全缺失 MDU / 托管 Wi-Fi 服务商这一档**。这一档形态显著不同——采购决策方是物业业主 / 开发商，不是 IT / 网络团队——其设计约束（全物业单一 SSID + 漫游、PropTech 集成、跨数百户的 RF 勘测）驱动着一种独特的硅片与特性需求，既不能直接套消费路由器，也不能直接套企业级 AP。这篇文章建立了该细分的规模（占美国新建多户约 9 成）、列出主要玩家，并给出可论证的经济机制（200–300 bps 的 NOI 提升）。配合运营商-MSO 的规模数据，它将成为"住宅物业里到底是谁买 Wi-Fi 7 / Wi-Fi 8 AP"以及"基于什么逻辑买"的基线参考。
