## Summary (EN)

Lee, Lee, Noh and Lee (August 2025, accepted to GLOBECOM 2025) study **Coordinated TDMA (Co-TDMA)** — one of the Multi-AP Coordination (MAPC) techniques on the table for 802.11bn — and quantify what it does for the kind of latency that actually matters for real-time applications: *worst-case latency* and *jitter*, rather than mean throughput.

The setup is system-level simulation of TGbn-aligned MAC behaviour with Co-TDMA scheduling for time-sensitive traffic. The headline result: **~24% reduction in worst-case latency** for low-latency traffic relative to the uncoordinated baseline, plus consistent reduction in jitter. Both numbers track directly with TGbn's three explicit performance targets (throughput +25% / p95 latency −25% / MPDU loss −25% — see `2026-05-01_ieee-tgbn-d2-schedule.md`), which makes Co-TDMA a credible mechanism for hitting the latency target.

The paper is the first quantitative case for Co-TDMA in the published TGbn literature and lands at a moment when the TGbn comment cluster has been actively debating MAPC scheduling between TWT-based and on-demand variants (see `2026-05-01_ieee-march-2026-readout.md`).

## Summary (ZH)

Lee、Lee、Noh、Lee（2025 年 8 月，被 GLOBECOM 2025 接收）研究**协同 TDMA（Co-TDMA）** —— 802.11bn 候选 MAPC 技术之一 —— 并量化其对实时应用真正关心的时延（*最坏时延*与*抖动*，而非平均吞吐）的影响。

实验设置是与 TGbn 一致的 MAC 行为的系统级仿真，对时延敏感流量启用 Co-TDMA 调度。关键结果：低时延流量的**最坏时延降低约 24%**，抖动也持续下降。两项数字都与 TGbn 的三项明确性能目标（吞吐 +25% / p95 时延 −25% / MPDU 丢包 −25%，见 `2026-05-01_ieee-tgbn-d2-schedule.md`）形成对应，使 Co-TDMA 成为命中时延目标的一个可信机制。

本论文是公开 TGbn 文献中第一篇 Co-TDMA 的定量论证，发表时正值 TGbn 评论集群正在 TWT 型与按需型 MAPC 调度之间激烈辩论（见 `2026-05-01_ieee-march-2026-readout.md`）。

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)

- **Quantitative anchor for Co-TDMA in TGbn debate.** TGbn comments have been arguing between TWT and on-demand MAPC scheduling without comparable numbers on either side. This paper supplies one for the TWT/coordinated branch.
- **Latency-as-metric continues to dominate.** Aligns with the WBA enterprise-trial focus on tail latency (`2026-05-01_wifi7-deployment-milestone-2026.md`) and the BLADE result (`2026-05-01_arxiv-blade-adaptive-contention.md`).

## Why it matters / what's new (ZH)

- **TGbn 辩论中 Co-TDMA 的定量锚点。** 此前 TGbn 评论在 TWT 与按需 MAPC 调度之间互不让步，但双方都缺乏可比数据。本论文为 TWT / 协同那一支提供了首个数据。
- **时延作为指标继续占主导。** 与 WBA 企业试验聚焦尾时延（`2026-05-01_wifi7-deployment-milestone-2026.md`）以及 BLADE 结果（`2026-05-01_arxiv-blade-adaptive-contention.md`）保持一致。
