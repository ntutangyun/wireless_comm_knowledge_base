## Summary (EN)

The Wi-Fi 8 announcements at MWC 2026 weren't only Qualcomm's — two second-tier-but-strategic announcements signal that the Wi-Fi 8 ecosystem is widening beyond the three big silicon vendors.

**Gemtek** (Taiwan-based ODM that supplies many tier-1 telco-CPE devices) used MWC 2026 to position Wi-Fi 8 as the foundational technology for "AI-driven, service-oriented networks". Their Wi-Fi 8 solutions advertise **deterministic low latency, carrier-grade reliability, multi-AP coordination, and AI-assisted optimisation**, with the explicit target of operator-deployed CPEs delivering immersive applications, real-time AI interaction, and large-scale residential and enterprise services.

**Quectel × MediaTek** unveiled an integrated **5G-Advanced + Wi-Fi 8 intelligent CPE reference design** built on MediaTek's T930 platform. The design pairs 5G-A (the 3GPP intermediate step toward 6G) cellular backhaul with Wi-Fi 8 access, targeting both residential broadband and enterprise / industrial-IoT use cases. The reference design is the cellular-side mirror image of what Broadcom (`2026-05-01_broadcom-wifi8-ces2026.md`) is doing on the residential gateway side.

Together, these announcements indicate that the Wi-Fi 8 product cycle is jumping straight to "integrated 5G-A + Wi-Fi 8" CPEs — operators can buy a single box that handles both fixed-wireless backhaul and indoor distribution.

**Gemtek**（一家台湾 ODM，供应大量一线运营商 CPE 设备）在 MWC 2026 上把 Wi-Fi 8 定位为"AI 驱动、面向业务"的网络基础技术。其 Wi-Fi 8 方案宣传**确定性低时延、运营商级可靠性、多 AP 协调以及 AI 辅助优化**，目标是运营商部署的 CPE 同时承载沉浸式应用、实时 AI 交互、大规模家庭与企业业务。

**Quectel × 联发科**联合发布了基于联发科 T930 平台的**集成 5G-Advanced + Wi-Fi 8 智能 CPE 参考设计**。该设计把 5G-A（3GPP 通往 6G 的过渡阶段）蜂窝回程与 Wi-Fi 8 接入配合，目标场景同时覆盖家庭宽带和企业 / 工业 IoT。该参考设计在蜂窝侧对应博通（`2026-05-01_broadcom-wifi8-ces2026.md`）在家庭网关侧所做的事。

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)

- **Ecosystem widening signal.** When ODMs (Gemtek) and module-makers (Quectel) announce Wi-Fi 8 at the same trade show as the silicon vendors, it means the operator-CPE supply chain is committing alongside the chipmakers — rather than waiting a generation.
- **Form-factor convergence.** The 5G-A + Wi-Fi 8 integrated CPE reference design is a strong product hypothesis. Telcos who have been deploying separate FWA modems and Wi-Fi APs now have a single-box upgrade path.
- **Watch list:** the next signal will be tier-1 telcos (AT&T, Verizon, BT, Deutsche Telekom, NTT) selecting Wi-Fi 8 CPE reference designs for their FWA programs in late 2026.
