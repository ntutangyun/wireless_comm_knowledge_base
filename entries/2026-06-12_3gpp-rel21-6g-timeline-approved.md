## Summary (EN)
At the TSGs#112 plenaries in Singapore (decision dated June 10, 2026), 3GPP formally approved the Release 21 timeline — the schedule for the first 3GPP release to contain normative 6G technical specifications. The timeline had been stable since it was first unveiled at the March 2026 plenaries, but is now an approved project anchor: working groups will match resources and feature prioritization to a Rel-21 functional (Stage-3) freeze at TSGs#122 in December 2028, with the ASN.1/OpenAPI freeze no earlier than March 2029.

The approved milestone ladder is: 5G-Advanced/6G package approval and Stage-1 freeze in March 2027; a Stage-2 80%-completion checkpoint in March 2028; Stage-2 freeze in June 2028; Stage-3 freeze in December 2028; and ASN.1/OpenAPI freeze in March 2029. The decision is recorded in plenary documents RP-260868 (RAN), SP-260595 (SA), and CP-261259 (CT). In parallel, the Rel-20 6G study programme continues feeding the normative phase: TSG RAN's Study on 6G Scenarios and Requirements (TR 38.914), SA2's 6G System architecture studies, and the RAN working-group 6G radio studies, with TR 22.870 ("6G Use Cases and Service Requirements", approved in Rel-20) serving as the Stage-1 anchor for Rel-21 normative work.

This closes the loop the KB has been tracking since March: the Rel-20/Rel-21 split (studies vs first 6G specs) now has hard dates on the normative side, aligned with the ITU IMT-2030 schedule. For chip, base-station, and device vendors, the December 2028 functional freeze and March 2029 protocol freeze open the concrete silicon-development window for first-wave 6G products around 2029–2030.

## Summary (ZH)
在新加坡举行的 TSGs#112 全会上（决议日期 2026 年 6 月 10 日），3GPP 正式批准了 Release 21 时间线——这是首个包含规范性 6G 技术规范的 3GPP 版本的日程表。该时间线自 2026 年 3 月全会首次公布以来保持稳定，如今成为获批的项目锚点：各工作组将按照 2028 年 12 月 TSGs#122 的 Rel-21 功能（Stage-3）冻结来匹配资源并进行特性优先级排序，ASN.1/OpenAPI 冻结不早于 2029 年 3 月。

获批的里程碑阶梯为：2027 年 3 月完成 5G-Advanced/6G 包批准与 Stage-1 冻结；2028 年 3 月 Stage-2 完成 80% 检查点；2028 年 6 月 Stage-2 冻结；2028 年 12 月 Stage-3 冻结；2029 年 3 月 ASN.1/OpenAPI 冻结。该决议记录于全会文件 RP-260868（RAN）、SP-260595（SA）与 CP-261259（CT）。与此同时，Rel-20 的 6G 研究计划继续为规范阶段供料：TSG RAN 的《6G 场景与需求研究》（TR 38.914）、SA2 的 6G 系统架构研究、RAN 工作组的 6G 无线研究，以及作为 Rel-21 规范工作 Stage-1 锚点的 TR 22.870（《6G 用例与业务需求》，已在 Rel-20 批准）。

这为本知识库自 3 月以来跟踪的线索画上句号：Rel-20/Rel-21 的分工（研究 vs 首批 6G 规范）如今在规范侧有了硬日期，并与 ITU IMT-2030 日程对齐。对芯片、基站与终端厂商而言，2028 年 12 月的功能冻结与 2029 年 3 月的协议冻结为 2029–2030 年前后的首波 6G 产品打开了具体的芯片研发窗口。

- Release 21 timeline formally approved at TSGs#112 (Singapore, June 10, 2026) — Rel-21 is the first 3GPP release with normative 6G specifications.
- Milestones: Stage-1 freeze March 2027 → Stage-2 80% checkpoint March 2028 → Stage-2 freeze June 2028 → Stage-3 / functional freeze December 2028 (TSGs#122) → ASN.1/OpenAPI freeze ≥ March 2029.
- Decision documents: RP-260868 (RAN), SP-260595 (SA), CP-261259 (CT).
- TR 22.870 (6G Use Cases and Service Requirements, Rel-20) is the Stage-1 anchor for Rel-21 normative work; TR 38.914 and SA2 6G architecture studies continue in parallel.
- Schedule aligned with ITU IMT-2030; timeline unchanged since its March 2026 unveiling, now binding for resource planning.
