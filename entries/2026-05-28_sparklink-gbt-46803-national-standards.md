## Summary (EN)
On 12 December 2025, China officially approved and released two new GB/T national standards for SparkLink (Huawei/Alliance branding: 星闪 / NearLink) short-distance wireless communication: **GB/T 46803.1-2025** and **GB/T 46803.2-2025**. Both standards cover the SLE (SparkLink Low Energy / 单链路扩展) profile — the low-power, low-cost branch of SparkLink that sits alongside the higher-throughput SLB (SparkLink Basic) profile. The release moves SparkLink SLE from the alliance / industry-spec layer (the existing `T/XS` series, e.g. `T/XS 10002` for SLE access-layer requirements) up to the formal national-standard layer, where compliance has regulatory weight in the Chinese market.

The two-part split is the conventional GB/T pattern for layered comms standards — typically Part 1 carries the architecture / general requirements and Part 2 specifies a particular layer (access / PHY / MAC) — but the per-Part technical scope was not surfaced in the public summaries available at backfill time; the standards' detailed text is gated behind the SAMR standards portal. What the public announcement does confirm is the milestone: GB/T 46803 is the first national-standard elevation of SparkLink content, intended to lead industry-standard development and provide "key technical support" for normalised, scaled deployment of SparkLink across vertical industries (consumer electronics, automotive digital keys, smart home, audio, robotics).

This sits in the broader SparkLink roadmap that the KB has been tracking: SparkLink 1.0 alliance standard (Nov 2022, dual-mode SLE/SLB), SparkLink 2.0 alliance standards (Mar 2024 series; Mar 2025 refresh adding audio + integrated sensing), the device-side rollouts on Pura 80 / Mate 70 / Watch 5 + the ICCE-SparkLink car-key wave (2026-05-24 entries), and the ecosystem position summarised in 2026-05-21_sparklink-ecosystem-2026-overview (1000–1200 alliance members, Chip-IP labs, OpenLab). GB/T 46803 is the regulatory / national-standard counterpart to that ecosystem buildout.

## Summary (ZH)
2025 年 12 月 12 日，中国正式批准发布两项 SparkLink（华为/联盟品牌：星闪 / NearLink）短距无线通信新国家标准：**GB/T 46803.1-2025** 与 **GB/T 46803.2-2025**。两项标准均针对 SLE（SparkLink Low Energy / 单链路扩展，俗称低功耗模式）档位——SparkLink 中与高吞吐 SLB（SparkLink Basic）并存的低功耗、低成本分支。本次发布把 SparkLink SLE 从联盟/团体标准层（已有的 `T/XS` 系列，例如规定 SLE 接入层要求的 `T/XS 10002`）升至正式国家标准层，使合规在中国市场具备监管效力。

按 GB/T 分层通信标准的惯例，两部分通常是"第 1 部分：架构/总则；第 2 部分：某一具体层（接入 / 物理层 / MAC）"——但在本回填整理时，公开摘要并未披露分部技术细节；标准全文需在国家标准信息公共服务平台（SAMR）查询。公开通告确认的核心要点是里程碑本身：GB/T 46803 是 SparkLink 内容首次以国家标准形式升格，旨在引领行业标准发展，为中国新一代短距无线通信生态在多行业（消费电子、汽车数字钥匙、智能家居、音频、机器人）的规模化与规范化部署提供"关键技术支撑"。

这与本 KB 已跟踪的 SparkLink 路线图相呼应：SparkLink 1.0 联盟标准（2022 年 11 月，SLE/SLB 双模）、SparkLink 2.0 联盟标准系列（2024 年 3 月；2025 年 3 月新增音频与通感一体）、设备端在 Pura 80 / Mate 70 / Watch 5 上的落地以及 ICCE-星闪车钥匙浪潮（2026-05-24 条目），以及 2026-05-21_sparklink-ecosystem-2026-overview 中归纳的生态格局（1000–1200 家联盟成员、芯片 IP 实验室、OpenLab）。GB/T 46803 是这一生态建设在监管/国家标准层面的对应物。

## Key technical points (EN)
- Two new GB/T national standards released 2025-12-12: **GB/T 46803.1-2025** and **GB/T 46803.2-2025**.
- Both target the **SLE (SparkLink Low Energy)** profile of SparkLink/NearLink — the low-power branch (paired with the higher-throughput SLB profile).
- **Elevation**: from alliance / industry-spec layer (existing T/XS series, e.g. T/XS 10002 for SLE access requirements) to formal national-standard layer (GB/T = recommended national standard).
- Two-part split follows the conventional GB/T pattern for comms standards (Part 1 = general / architecture, Part 2 = specific layer); per-Part scope **not** surfaced in public summaries at backfill time — the SAMR portal carries the full text.
- Intent (per official announcements): lead industry-standard development; enable normalised, scaled cross-industry deployment (consumer, auto digital keys, smart home, audio, robotics).
- Sits alongside: SparkLink 1.0 (Nov 2022 alliance), SparkLink 2.0 (Mar 2024 / Mar 2025 refresh w/ audio + ISAC), the 2026 device-side rollout (Pura 80 / Mate 70 / Watch 5 / ICCE-SparkLink car key).
