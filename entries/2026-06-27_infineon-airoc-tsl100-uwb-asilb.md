## Summary (EN)
Infineon has launched the AIROC TSL100, described in the press coverage as the first UWB chip targeted at automotive safety use cases. The reported hook is an **ASIL-B** safety positioning, which matters because automotive UWB is moving beyond convenience access and digital key toward functions that interact with vehicle safety and battery systems.

The chip is framed around wireless battery management and in-vehicle/vehicle-adjacent UWB use. Wireless battery management systems replace some wired harnessing inside battery packs with short-range wireless links; for that role, a UWB implementation has to provide robust ranging or localization, reliable data exchange, predictable coexistence, and a safety case that automotive integrators can audit. Infineon's AIROC branding also places TSL100 beside the company's broader connectivity portfolio rather than as a standalone niche radio.

This entry complements the KB's existing 802.15.4ab commercialization thread. The Ceva entry covers licensable UWB IP and STMicroelectronics covers early 4ab silicon; Infineon's TSL100 adds an explicitly automotive-safety product angle, with wireless battery management as the lead deployment story.

## Summary (ZH)

该芯片围绕无线电池管理以及车内/车旁 UWB 使用场景展开。无线电池管理系统用短距无线链路替代电池包内部的部分线束；在这一角色中，UWB 实现需要提供稳健的测距或定位、可靠数据交换、可预测共存，以及可供汽车集成商审计的安全论证。AIROC 品牌也表明 TSL100 是英飞凌更广连接产品组合的一部分，而不是孤立的小众无线电。

该条目补足了 KB 中已有的 802.15.4ab 商业化线索。Ceva 条目覆盖可授权 UWB IP，STMicroelectronics 条目覆盖早期 4ab 硅片；英飞凌 TSL100 则加入了明确的汽车安全产品视角，并以无线电池管理作为核心部署故事。

## Why it matters / what's new (EN)
UWB automotive coverage often stops at digital key and hands-free access. TSL100 is notable because it puts automotive safety and battery management at the center, suggesting UWB is being productized for internal vehicle subsystems as well as user-facing access. That expands the UWB-ranging bin from secure proximity toward safety-qualified in-vehicle sensing and telemetry.
